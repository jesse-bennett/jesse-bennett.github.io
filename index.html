About

Hi, I'm Jesse!

I'm a Computer Science student at TMU with a passion for coding and game development. I've been coding since I was 10 years old, and I like to think of it as a creative outlet, an art form. When I was very young I used to dream about a world of dragons and castles, a story of a young boy who takes on the world, a big adventure. I've poured out much of my free time the last couple years into Skydom, the realization of that dream.

I'm wrapping up my Senior year at TMU pursuing a Bachlor's in Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence. I'm a Software Engineer at Viasat in Carlsbad but I still make time for Game Development wherever I can.
